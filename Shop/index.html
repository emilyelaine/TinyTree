Shop

We have a variety of patterns and knitted goods available for you. Each pattern and product can be created in a variety of sizes. We also love trying new things so visit our special requests page and let us know what you are looking for.

Patterns

Peruse our selection of custom patterns available for sale! Each pattern has sizing for a newborn up to 2 years. Hats however, can be created for up to a 5 year old. Take a look and send us a picture of your final projects. We love to see your work!

Special Requests

Don't see what you are looking for? Send us a message and we will see what we can do for you. Please note that our turn around for custom orders can be between 1-2 months, depending on the number of current requests.
