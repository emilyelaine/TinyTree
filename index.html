Who We Are

Welcome to Tiny Tree Knits! We make and design a wide range of baby/toddler sized knit garments. Each piece is hand crafted with your little one in mind. We use high quality yarns that will be less likely to irritate your child's skin and are machine washable!

Our Products

Lions and tigers and bears, oh my we do make them all! Pick from a variety of patterns including some of our recent designs; dinosaurs, frogs, and tigers. We are open to new ideas so if you have something in mind, contact us! We'll see what we can come up with.
